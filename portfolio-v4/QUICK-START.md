### Fastest Deploy (No Command Line)

1. **Go to GitHub**
   - Visit: `github.com/[your-username]/portfolio`

2. **Upload Files**
   - Click **Add file** → **Upload files**
   - Drag and drop all 4 files
   - Commit: "Portfolio v4"

3. **Enable GitHub Pages**
   - **Settings** → **Pages**
   - Source: **main** branch
   - Click **Save**

4. **Done!**
   - Live in 2 minutes at: `[your-username].github.io/portfolio`
